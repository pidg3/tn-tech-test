With stunning views around the park the charming Birch Lodges are the perfect place to escape and unwind.
Situated on the outskirts of Newton Stewart, with the backdrop of the Galloway Forest Park on one side and bordered by the Golf Course on the other, this is the ideal base for a family holiday or a romantic break. The forest itself is a paradise for nature lovers and within easy reach of superb fishing, walking and mountain biking.
Birch Lodges sleep up to 4 in one King Bedroom and one Twin Bedroom with a family bathroom. The Birch Lodges boast a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher which opens out an open plan dining/living room (with TV and DVD player). Each lodge has an enclosed decking around the front and side with featuring patio furniture.
